## Key Messaging Principles

- Lead with the recipient, not the sender — "your family gets it" not "you send it"
- Emotion first, features second — connection before transaction
- Proof over claims — every assertion backed by a number
- WhatsApp is a differentiator — surface it, don't bury it
- Cash pickup is the moat — 600K locations vs. digital-only competitors
- 35 years = credibility no startup can fake
